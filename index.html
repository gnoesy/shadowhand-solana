ShadowHand Demo · Hidden-state onchain gameplay

Play a private card round without revealing your hand.

This demo shows the core idea behind ShadowHand: your hand stays hidden, your move is processed privately, and only the rule-required reveal appears at the end. It is a simple front-end prototype for demonstrating confidential game execution with Arcium MXE.
